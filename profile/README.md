## ✦ Our mission

Modern AI assistants are powerful — but most of them ship your most private moments (your meetings, your notes, your thoughts) to someone else's servers. We think that trade-off is unnecessary. Apple Silicon is fast enough to run capable AI models locally, so that's exactly what we do. Your data never leaves your Mac.

## 🔒 Why on-device?

| | ☁️ Cloud AI | 🍏 Bitwize.ai |
| --- | :---: | :---: |
| Where your data lives | On someone's servers | **On your Mac, only** |
| Meeting capture | Requires a bot to join | **System audio — no bot** |
| Transcription limits | Metered / capped | **Unlimited** |
| Works offline | ❌ | **✅** |
| Monthly subscription | Usually required | **Free tier, no fees** |
| Your privacy | You're the product | **You're the customer** |

## 🧩 Open source at Bitwize

We build in the open where it matters. **[Logue](https://github.com/bitwize-ai/Logue) is fully open source** under the [MIT license](https://github.com/bitwize-ai/Logue/blob/main/LICENSE) — the same app we ship, source and all. If you care about private, local-first AI on Apple platforms, we'd love your stars, issues, and pull requests.
